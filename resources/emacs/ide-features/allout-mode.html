Allout-mode

Set up: 

(require 'allout)
(allout-init t)

All comments preceded by comment chars (;;;_ used by allout.el for emacs lisp). By default, stylish prefixes are enabled. 

This is how an outline can look (but sans indentation) with stylish
prefixes:

    * Top level
    .* A topic
    . + One level 3 subtopic
    .  . One level 4 subtopic
    .  . A second 4 subtopic
    . + Another level 3 subtopic
    .  #1 A numbered level 4 subtopic
    .  #2 Another
    .  ! Another level 4 subtopic with a different distinctive bullet
    .  #4 And another numbered level 4 subtopic

This would be an outline with stylish prefixes inhibited (but the
numbered and other distinctive bullets retained):

    * Top level
    .* A topic
    . * One level 3 subtopic
    .  * One level 4 subtopic
    .  * A second 4 subtopic
    . * Another level 3 subtopic
    .  #1 A numbered level 4 subtopic
    .  #2 Another
    .  ! Another level 4 subtopic with a different distinctive bullet
    .  #4 And another numbered level 4 subtopic

Symbols used in allout.el 

;;;_ * asdf
;;;_  > asdf
;;;_  + asdf
;;;_   = asdf
;;;_  + asdf
;;;_   = asdf
;;;_   : adsf
;;;_ * asdf
;;;_  #1 asdf
;;;_   : asdf
;;;_    = asdf
;;;_  #2 adsf

Keybindings when cursor is in correct position (no prefix needed) 

b outline-backward-current-level
f outline-forward-current-level
h outline-hide-current-subtree
i outline-show-children
n outline-next-visible-heading
o outline-show-current-entry
p outline-previous-visible-heading
s outline-show-current-subtree
u outline-up-current-level

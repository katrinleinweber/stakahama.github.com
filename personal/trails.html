GPS

I have two GPS devices:

  • Android phone (uploads directly to Google Maps). Has camera with geotagging. Battery life ~5 hours.
  • Garmin 301 Forerunner (generates .gpx through modern Garmin Training Center). Battery life ~13 hours.

Advantage of archiving runs in Google Maps rather than Google Earth: sharable online; switch views. Google Maps can import .kml files. Need to convert .gpx to .kml with Google Earth or {…}.
